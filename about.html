About TLE

What is TLE?

TLE is an online Discord community for students to chat with native English speakers, and other English learners, to practice and improve their Engish skills with free English learning resources, a variety of English courses, English blogs for students, and English podcasts and videos to take your English slills to the next level.

TLE Events

The TLE community has many free events to help you improve your English skills. Native English speakers will have live streams to teach vocabulary and speaking skills, host live Q&A sessions for students to ask English questions, and weekly English quizzes and games, and English video watch parties.
